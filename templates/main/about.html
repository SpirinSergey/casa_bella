B&B Italia

CasaBella Residences by B&B Italia have collaborated with this iconic brand to bring the exquisite experience of Italian design to life in Miami.

Founded in 1966 by Piero Ambrogio Busnelli, B&B Italia stands out both for its representation of contemporary culture as well as its research and innovation, which has allowed the brand to create products with unique style and artistry. The fruitful partnership between the company’s Research & Development Center, a workshop for ideas and excellence, and the best international design professionals has resulted in the creation of iconic products which have radically helped define the history of design. B&B Italia has been honored with numerous awards in its history, including a four-time win of the most coveted award in Italian industrial design: the “Compasso d’Oro.”

Design for Life

Casa B&B Italia will host a discerning collection of celebrated contemporary artwork and sculpture by Italian masters.
